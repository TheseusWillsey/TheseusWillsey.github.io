Overview


This is a card-based board game for 2, expandable to multiple people. Players try to advance to the end of the track by competing with cards.


Gameplay

The game is played in rounds. Each player places two roles down, one in the Primary position and one in the Secondary.


The two players compare the power of their Primaries, backed by the abilities of the Secondaries, and the resulting difference in power advances their piece along the track.


Future Development


This game can be expanded to include multiple people trying to push their pieces along the track, maybe including a hidden role mechanic. Definitely more players is a development goal.
